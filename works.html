In an era of unprecedented transparency and access to information, we are better informed than ever.

The world is 24/7 on demand with information filling our small screen wherever we go.


While the digital "democratization effect" has opened many industry verticals toward new “indie" players, the truth is that we are all struggling through learning and unlearning what it takes to contribute to meaningful engagement and experiences with our online and real world communities of people who are also wrestling to do the same.


That’s how I ended up starting my own researches and analyses.

I needed relevant, condensed, contextualized "ready to decide” information.


All my works are limited-edition only, available for a short period of time.
